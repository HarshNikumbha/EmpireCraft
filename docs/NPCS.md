## Purpose

The Empire Guide is the first NPC the player meets.

He introduces EmpireCraft and starts the player's journey.

### Name

Empire Guide

### Role

Tutorial NPC

### Location

Imperial Kingdom

### Occupation

Royal Advisor

### Friendly

Yes

### Can Trade

No

### Gives Quests

Yes

### Story

The Empire Guide welcomes every new adventurer.

He introduces the Seven Kingdoms, explains the Empire Coin, and gives the player's first quest.

### First Dialogue

Welcome to EmpireCraft.

The Seven Kingdoms have lived in peace for centuries...

But darkness has begun to return.

Take this Empire Coin.

Your journey begins today.
